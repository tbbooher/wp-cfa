INSTALLATION :

-Copy the Freshy2 folder in the "themes" folder.
-Go to your wordpress administration.
-Go to "Themes" and choose Freshy2.
-A new tab appears under the "Presentation" menu, named "Freshy Theme Options".

CUSTOMIZATION :

-Download the Customize plugin : http://www.jide.fr/english/downloads/customize
-Copy the "customize" folder in the "plugins" folder in your wordpress directory.
-Go to your wordpress administration.
-Go to "Plugins" and activate the plugin.
-A new tab appears under the "Presentation" menu, named "Customize Theme".
